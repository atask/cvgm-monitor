   <h2><img class="icon_header" src="http://www.cvgm.net/static/flags/invader.png" alt="" /><span class="headline"> Welcome To CVGM!</span></h2>
   <div class="newstext">Welcome to CVGM, your home for video game, demo scene and computer related music. We stream 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year for your listening pleasure! This site is devoted to the men and women who devote their time creating some of the best music available in the world!<br /><br />You can immediately begin listening to the music by heading over to the <a href="http://www.cvgm.net/demovibes/streams/">Audio Streams</a> page, and selecting a stream that's best for you. We have several different streams to choose from. Alternatively, you can click "Listen" in your browser and select one of the streams from the list to listen directly in your browser!<br /><br />If you haven't done so already, please register on the site. It's quick, and doing so will enable you to do many new things including:<br /><br /><ul><li>Personal Favourites List</li><li>Request songs to be played on the stream.</li><li>Chat with other members in the OneLiner</li><li>Vote for songs you like/dislike the most.</li><li>Create new artists and groups that don't yet exist.</li><li>Upload songs not currently on the site.</li><li>Make corrections to existing songs.</li><li>Send messages to other members of the site.</li><li>Post on our forums, comment on songs, and lot's more!.</li></ul><br /><br />To familiarize yourself with the many features of the site, we have posted <a href="/forum/thread/44/"><img src="/static/newspaper.png" alt="forum" border="0" /> Getting Started With CVGM</a> to help get you started!<br /><br />We hope you enjoy your stay on our site! If you have any questions or comments, you can post on our forums, ask in the OneLiner, or send a message to any moderator/admin or email us at demovibes [at] cvgm.net!<br /><br />To see some of the new albums/song sets uploaded to CVGM, see <img src="/static/newspaper.png" alt="forum" border="0"><a href="/forum/thread/84/">Recently Added Albums/Compilations</a>.    <h2><img class="icon_header" src="/static/newspaper.png" alt="" /><span class="headline"> Server Changes Are Now Complete!</span> <span class="date">
 
 25 April 2014
 
 <span class="tzinfo">23:03</span>

</span></h2>
   <div class="newstext">Its been a while since we posted any news about whats been going on with the site, so I thought I would just give a bit of an update to everyone about where things are. <br /><br />We have completed the migration to the new VMWare server. This is a server that was purchased as an upgrade to the existing CVGM servers, and to give us the expandability we may need in the future as the site grows bigger. In the beginning, CVGM consisted of 2 servers, an ancient Xeon server with 2gb of ram to run the Python code, and an even more ancient AMD dual core server to handle the streams. When these boxes started to break down (they were leased from the datacenter we use) we ended up purchasing our own hardware, with the help of several donations to the site from our awesome listeners.<br /><br />The new server has 8 3.16Ghz cores and 16gb of ram. It also allows us to split up the resources a little better, so the streams can now have their own little server etc. All good things :) For more details on the technical changes to the site, you can read up on one of these forum threads:<br /><br /><img src="/static/newspaper.png" alt="forum" border="0" /> <a href="/forum/thread/238/">New Server Changes</a><br /><img src="/static/newspaper.png" alt="forum" border="0" /> <a href="/forum/thread/239/">New Icecast Streams!</a><br /><br />As always, if you have any questions or concerns, feel free to jump on the oneliner and ask, or post a message in the forums. Thanks!<br /><br />FishGuy876<br /></div>
